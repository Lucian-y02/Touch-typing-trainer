Try to see others as an extension of yourself. We are all in this life experience together. Knowing that we are all connected is practicing universal kindness, for sure.
Say "thank you" when someone holds the door or elevator for you.
Say "thank you" to people who go beyond the regular call of duty to help you and serve your interests.
Say "thank you" to your teachers and instructors who have educated and helped you to reach your professional goals.
Say "thank you" to your parents and relatives who always wish the very best for you, despite their own troubles.
Say "thank you" to all friends and colleagues who have looked out for you over the years and who still enjoy your company.
